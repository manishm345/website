Manish Maheshwari

AI Entrepreneur & Mason Fellow at Harvard

Professional Profile

Manish Maheshwari is a seasoned tech executive, entrepreneur, and AI expert currently a Mason Fellow at Harvard University, specializing in AI's social impact and public policy. He co-founded Fanory.ai, an AI-driven Creator Economy startup, and previously served as Twitter India's Country Head, driving rapid user and revenue growth.

With an MBA (Honors) from The Wharton School, backed by a Ford Research Fellowship, he began his career at McKinsey & Company in New York. A TEDx speaker, MENSA member, and PADI-certified advanced diver, Manish thrives on innovation, leadership, and creating sustainable value in dynamic markets.
